Giraffe Delegate

Giraffe Delegate is aimed to be a delegate providing SmartBridges service.

We plan to provide: 1. Contract execution service, using ARK to invoke Ethereum Smart Contract. 2. Cryptocurrencies exchange service, starting with ARK-to-ETH and ARK-to-Tokens(ERC20).

Please see our proposal for detail explanation.

How to use?

This site demonstrates the ability to exchange ARK(testnet) to ETH(Rinkeby testnet) through SmartBridges.

You just need to send ARK(testnet) to DKpAhq5QNVGKWLwnFFe9EJfQxFrfza4E8e, and including SmartBridges Data: {"eth": "your-ethereum-account"}.

When we receive your ARK transaction, we will send ETH to your Ethereum acccount. The ETH amount is fixed to 0.0001 ETH(Rinkeby) for now.
